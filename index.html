About Us

SYFFAH, which stands for 'Share Your Food, Feed All Humanity' is a Non-Profit Organization working in New York, USA. Established in January 2021, our dedicated team works endlessly towards a more sustainable and equitable future.

Tackling Food Waste

Our website connects food donors such as individuals with leftover food, event caterers with excess food, grocery store-keepers etc. to food collectors like NGOs and Hunger Relief Organizations who can distribute the food among needy so that no food goes into the drains while millions sleep hungry.

Our Mission

SYFFAH aims to reduce food wastage and E-wastage which are major contributers to solid waste in NYC, while simultaneously combating issues of poverty and hunger in NYC, which have been further aggravated post COVID-19 pandemic. Thus, tackling 5 of the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals simultaneously.

Tackling E-Waste

Through our website individuals, shops, etc. can donate their old/second hand refrigerator instead of throwing it away to landfills. We then repair these refrigerators and set them up across the city so that people can keep leftover food in it and underprivileged people can take that food to eat. These are hence called Community refrigerators.
